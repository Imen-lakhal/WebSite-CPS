The Tunisian Red Crescent is an organization of public interest, created on October 7, 1956, and recognized by decree of May 6, 1957 as a voluntary organization of aid and aid auxiliary to the public authorities in the humanitarian field, in accordance with the 1949 Geneva Conventions and its Additional Protocols. The Tunisian Red Crescent became a member of the International Red Cross and Red Crescent Movement on 13 September 1957
